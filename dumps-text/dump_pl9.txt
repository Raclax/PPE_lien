   noun
     [ masculine ] /zvjɔ̃zɛk/
   Add to word list Add to word list
   ●

   topic.

   noun

   compound  [noun] a substance, word etc formed from two or more elements
   connection  [noun] (a) state of being connected or related
   fellowship  [noun] an association (of people with common interests)
   relation  [noun] a relationship (between facts, events etc).
