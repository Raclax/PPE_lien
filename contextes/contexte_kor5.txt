관계

   /gwangye/
--
   A bond between people or groups is a close connection that they have
   with each other, for example because they have a special agreement.
   ...the strong bond between church and nation. 교회와 국가 간의 밀접한 관계
   2. connection
   variable noun
   A connection is a relationship between two things, people, or groups.
   There was no evidence of a connection between BSE and the brain
   diseases recently confirmed in cats. 광우병과 최근 고양이에서 확인된 뇌질환 사이의 관계를 뒷받침할
   증거가 없었다., I felt a strong connection between us. 나는 우리 사이에 강한 유대감을 느꼈다.
   3. link
--
   business connection between them.
   He hopes to cement close links between Northern Ireland and India. 그는
   북아일랜드와 인도가 친밀한 관계를 다지기를 바란다., He also emphasized that there must be a
   close link between the prison and the community which it serves. 그는 감옥과
   그 감옥 구성원들간의 긴밀한 친분 관계가 있다고도 강조했다.
   4. relationship
   countable noun
--
   We had been together for two years, but both of us felt the
   relationship wasn't really going anywhere. 우리는 2년 동안 함께 지냈으나, 우리 둘 다
   관계가 진척되고 있다는 느낌은 없었다.
   5. relationship
   countable noun
--
   feel and behave toward each other.
   ...the friendly relationship between France and Britain. 프랑스와 영국 간의 우호
   관계
   6. relation
   countable noun
--
   about the ways in which they are connected.
   It is a question of the relation of ethics to economics. 그것은 윤리와 경제와의
   관계에 대한 문제이다.
   7. relation
   countable noun
--
   them and the way in which they behave toward each other.
   Greece has established full diplomatic relations with Israel. 그리스는
   이스라엘과 완전한 외교 관계를 수립했다.
