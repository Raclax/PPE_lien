Link time

Updated: 11/12/2023 by Computer Hope

Link time may refer to any of the following:

1. With programming, link time is the time it takes a linker to create an executable program from compiled modules like programs, routines, or libraries.

2. When describing a software or hardware connection, link time is the length of time it takes a program or device to establish a connection or sync.
